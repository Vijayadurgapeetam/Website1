Temple History

Sri Vijaya Durga Peetam is a spiritual center established in the village of Vedurupaka in the present day Raivaram Mandal, Ramachandrapuram taluka, East Godavari district. In the year 1974, the Peetam was hailed by the Vijayadurga Peethadhipati, who took a decision with his meticulous observation, ingenuity in problem solving activities, Vedic knowledge, Mantra Deeksha Tithi and weekly astrological calculations at the meeting of mystics and devotees. The pedestal was established with the sacred figures of Sri Chakra, Astabhujavyagravahini, Dakshinavrutha shanka, Eswarabanam, Ganapathi which are made from Panchaloha in the year 1972 …

God Garu
